Event Announcement

Semester Hike (Goldau - Unterägeri)

Event Date: 6 May 2023 8am - 6pm
Location: Goldau
Coordinators: Radim Urban, Aleš Kubíček

Let us know you're in! CSSZ invites you and your friends to join a semester hike from Goldau-Unterägeri, which is planned for Saturday, 06.05.2023. Come and enjoy hiking in the beautiful nature with our society!

Difficulty

Easy to sporty - about 1000m up and 850m down - but only 15.6km in total.

Transport connection

We’ll take a direct train to Goldau and we’ll take a bus and train on the way back from Unterägeri (ca. 1:15, one transfer). See more on our webpage!
