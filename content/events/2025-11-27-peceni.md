**Christmas Gingerbread Baking**

As is tradition in the Czech and Slovak republics before Christmas, we will be baking gingerbread in various sizes and shapes. Come and join us for an evening full of creativity, mulled wine and Christmas atmosphere!

## **Registration and Prices**

Registration is required as the event is limited to 20 participants. Price includes ingredients to make gingerbread cookies and drinks.

- **Active CSSZ Members** (enrolled in any BSc/MSc/PhD program): CHF 5
- **Other CSSZ Members**: CHF 10
- **External**: CHF 15

Please register as soon as possible if you are planning on coming to the event.

We look forward to sharing a delightful evening with you!
